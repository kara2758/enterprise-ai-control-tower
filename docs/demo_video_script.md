# Demo Video Script (60–90 seconds)

*Voiceover script for a screen-recording walkthrough of the Enterprise AI Control
Tower. Estimated run time: ~75 seconds at a natural pace.*

> Tip: record the dashboard at `http://localhost:8501` and scroll through the
> sections in the order below. Keep the pace calm and executive.

---

### Opening (0:00–0:08)

> "This is the Enterprise AI Control Tower — an AI-powered decision-support
> prototype that gives executives a single, real-time view of business health.
> Everything you'll see runs on synthetic data."

### Business Problem (0:08–0:18)

> "Most leaders don't lack data — they lack a timely, interpreted view of it.
> Finance, support, and approvals live in separate systems, and risks are often
> caught too late. This dashboard brings those signals together."

### Dashboard Overview (0:18–0:30)

> "At the top, executive KPI cards summarize open work, items needing attention,
> total financial exposure, SLA pressure, and process bottlenecks — the headline
> health of the business at a glance."

### KPI and Risk Engine (0:30–0:45)

> "Behind these numbers, a KPI engine computes metrics across invoices, tickets,
> and approvals. A risk engine then applies clear business rules to flag and
> prioritize issues — each finding with a severity score and a recommended
> action. The charts and the top-risks table make the hotspots obvious."

### AI Executive Summary (0:45–0:57)

> "With one click, the AI Executive Summary turns all of this into a
> management-ready briefing: a short narrative, key risks, recommended actions,
> and questions for the leadership meeting. It runs in demo mode without any API
> key, or in live mode using OpenAI."

### AI Manager Assistant (0:57–1:08)

> "The AI Manager Assistant lets you ask questions in plain language — like
> 'Which department needs attention first?' or 'Summarize approval bottlenecks' —
> and answers grounded only in the KPI and risk data."

### Weekly Automation Report (1:08–1:18)

> "Finally, an n8n automation demo shows how a weekly executive report could be
> generated and prepared for delivery automatically — safely, with no real
> emails sent."

### Closing (1:18–1:25)

> "Data to KPIs to risk to AI-driven decision support — modular, reproducible,
> and built as a professional prototype. Thanks for watching."

---

*Reminder: this is a portfolio prototype on synthetic data, not a production
system. Avoid claiming real deployments or measured savings in the recording.*
